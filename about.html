About the SMILE

SMILE Travel & Tours Company is established in 2010. Proudly founded in Yangon, we offer several destinations all around the world. SMILE has offered unforgettable and valuable travel memories to more than 40,000 tourists. We offer all-in-one packages which include comfortable transport and accommodation, delicious local food, tour guides, game shows and many other interesting things. SMILE has been the most popular tour company in Myanmar over a decade.
